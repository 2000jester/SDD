Upon entering the "Test your speed" page you will be met with some stats on the left of the page. Once you start the minigame it will start recording the total amount of characters and words you have typed, it will also calculate your words per minute"
Once you press "enter" to start the minigame you will be prompted with a random word, which upon typing correctly will generate a new word for you to type. The goal is to type as many words as you can in the provided minute before the minigame resets.
